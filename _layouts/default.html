About Me

I'm an Operations Analyst at CPower Energy on a mission to step into Data Science leadership.

With a Master's in Public Policy (Columbia) and 12 months of full-stack energy data automation (2–3 million rows/month per database), I specialize in:

  • Building Python pipelines & Azure SQL systems for ISO-NE, PJM, ERCOT, CAISO, MISO
  • Developing Power BI dashboards adopted by analysts, sales teams, and engineers
  • Forecasting load with neural-net models and crafting real-time CO₂ emissions indices
  • Integrating CI/CD (GitHub Actions), containerization (Docker), and MLOps best practices
  • Collaborating cross-functionally to replace legacy processes with lean, scalable tools

When I'm not automating data flows, you'll find me tinkering with new forecasting architectures, exploring Jupyter-centric MLOps, or walking my dogs around NYC.
